# 🛒 Kirti Firm E-commerce Sales Dashboard

## 1. Project Title
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze e-commerce sales performance, customer behavior, and profit trends across regions and categories.

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose
This dashboard provides insights into sales, profit, quantity, and customer trends for an e-commerce business. It helps identify high-performing states, profitable product categories, and seasonal trends in revenue.

---

## 3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using:

- 📊 Power BI Desktop – Data visualization and dashboard creation  
- 📂 Excel – Data source and basic preprocessing  
- 📈 Built-in Power BI Charts – Bar charts, pie charts, and line charts for analysis  
- 🧩 Filters & Slicers – For interactive data exploration  

---

## 4. Data Source
- Sample e-commerce dataset (Excel file)
- Includes:
  - Customer Name  
  - State  
  - Category & Sub-category  
  - Payment Mode  
  - Sales Amount, Profit, Quantity  

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem
E-commerce businesses generate large amounts of sales data, but extracting meaningful insights about profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance is difficult without proper visualization.

---

### • Goal of the Dashboard
- Track overall business performance  
- Identify profitable products and regions  
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns  
- Monitor monthly profit trends  

---

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### 🔹 KPIs (Top Section)
- Total Sales: 387K  
- Total Profit: 31K  
- Total Quantity: 4825  
- Average Order Value: 107K  

---

#### 🔹 Sales by State
Shows which states contribute the most revenue, with Maharashtra being the top contributor.

---

#### 🔹 Quantity by Category
- Clothing contributes the highest share (~62%)  
- Electronics and Furniture follow  

---

#### 🔹 Profit by Month
- High profits observed in January to March  
- Losses observed in May, July, and December  

---

#### 🔹 Customer-wise Sales
Highlights top customers contributing the most to overall revenue.

---

#### 🔹 Payment Mode Distribution
- COD is the most used payment method (~44%)  
- Followed by UPI, Debit, Credit, and EMI  

---

#### 🔹 Profit by Sub-category
- Printers generate the highest profit  
- Tables and accessories show comparatively lower performance  

---

### • Business Impact & Insights
- 📈 Focus marketing efforts on high-performing states like Maharashtra  
- 💸 Investigate losses in specific months and optimize pricing or costs  
- 🛍️ Promote high-profit products like printers  
- 💳 Encourage digital payments to reduce dependency on COD
